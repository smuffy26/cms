I'm lost take me home

This site is based on a publication — a documentation of my grandmothers and great grandmothers recipes — a publication which aims to ensure that family recipes will be passed down through the generations and not lost.


This site contains a collection of content found through google searches in order to mimick the trail that is left behind when something is lost.


This site requires viewers to scroll down and across the page in order to find the images. The viewer may find they are staring at a blank patch of the site only to scroll and uncover more overlapping images.


This site is created to ensure that lost objects/people/places/religions/countries that were once special to someone, that once held significant meaning or stories are not forgotten.
